Score: 79/100

Citability 21/25, Structural Readability 18/20, Multi-Modal Content 8/15, Authority & Brand Signals 13/20, Technical Accessibility 19/20 — the two highest-leverage gaps from the 2026-07-19 audit (no llms.txt, no FAQ content/schema) are both resolved. Remaining gaps are now secondary: thin multi-modal content (no comparison tables, no video), weak off-site entity/brand signals, and no freshness/author signals on product pages.

## What Works

- **robots.txt remains fully permissive**: `User-Agent: * / Allow: /` with only `/cart` and `/search` disallowed — confirmed via curl. No explicit blocks for GPTBot, OAI-SearchBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, or any other AI crawler.
- **llms.txt is now live** at `/llms.txt` (HTTP 200): well-structured with a one-line brand/entity description, unit counts per category, all 8 category links with counts, 4 blog guide links, contact info, and a sitemap pointer for the full catalog. This is a strong implementation, not a bare-minimum stub.
- **FAQPage schema + visible Q&A content now ship on every product page**: confirmed via raw curl (no JS needed) on `/product/10ft-refrigerated-container-10ft-freezer` — a real `<h2>Common Questions</h2>` block with five `<h3>` question headings (condition, deposit/reservation process, delivery cost, return policy, Section 179 tax write-off) each followed by a self-contained 1-3 sentence answer, plus matching `FAQPage`/`Question`/`Answer` JSON-LD in the page `<head>`. Answers are policy-grounded (not fabricated) per the source comment in `src/lib/faq.ts`.
- **Question-form headings now exist**: `<h2>Common Questions</h2>` + five question-phrased `<h3>`s render in the raw server HTML — directly what AI Overviews/ChatGPT favor for direct-answer extraction. Previously all headings were generic labels.
- **JSON-LD product image field is now clean**: `"image":["https://www.containeronedepot.com/images/...jpg", ...]` — a proper array of well-formed URLs; the previously reported double-domain concatenation bug was not reproduced on the spot-checked page.
- **New long-form guide content**: 4 blog posts now exist (container grades explained, Section 179 deduction, reefer buying guide, deposit/reservation process) with `BlogPosting` JSON-LD including `datePublished`, adding citable, topic-specific pages beyond product listings.
- SSR confirmed unchanged: raw curl (no JS) returns full product copy, spec `<dl>` lists, FAQ block, and all JSON-LD in the initial HTML response.
